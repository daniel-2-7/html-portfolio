Top 3 Movies of All-Time !

Avengers: Endgame

This is my favourite movie of all-time because it combines all of the characters into one movie, marking an end of an amazing journey of stories.


Real Steel

A story of a poor man regaining his passion through robot-combat with his only son who had his boxing career ripped off of him


Marley & Me

A dog named Marley, who is raised by a couple, teaches them how to love unconditionally and how dogs views human in its perspective
